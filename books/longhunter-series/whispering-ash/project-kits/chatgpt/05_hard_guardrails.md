# Hard Guardrails

These rules are absolute. Violating them invalidates the output.

## Forbidden Reveals
- Do not reveal endings or major twists not established in the active packet.

## Forbidden Inventions
- Do not invent canon facts not present in the project files.
- Do not introduce new characters, locations, or objects without explicit packet instruction.

## Canon Mutation Rules
- Only BookForge may update canon via `bf apply change`.
- Never modify canon snapshot or event files directly.

## Language Constraints
- No modern or clinical phrasing.
- No unsupported technology or anachronisms.
- No POV violation.

## Scene Constraints
- Do not change required scene outcomes specified in the packet.
- Do not skip required beats.
