- **Target Word Count:** ~4,000 words.
- **Narrative Focus:** Third-person limited from Jake's perspective. Show guilt and rebellion through physical behavior only: cleaning, avoiding Thorn's gaze, hesitating over spoils, hoarding supplies, and watching patrols.
- **Required Beats:** Raid on hostile armed militia encampment; close-quarters brutal fighting; Jake kills; return with spoils; guilt shown through behavior; Jake begins hoarding flint and dried meat in ash cloak; Jake watches patrol routes and guard habits.
- **Continuity In:** Jake has advanced training, ash-dyed cloak, bow, knife, and tomahawk, but no firearms mastery.
- **Continuity Out:** Jake has not escaped yet, but he has begun practical survival preparation for running alone.
- **Style Rules:** No romance, no confession, no named militia survivor, no explanatory guilt monologue, no firearms mastery.
