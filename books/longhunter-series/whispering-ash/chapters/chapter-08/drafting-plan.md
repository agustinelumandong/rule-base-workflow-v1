# Chapter 8 Drafting Plan

- **Target Word Count:** ~3,900 words.
- **Narrative Focus:** Third-person limited from Jake's perspective. Show earned status through physical reaction and village treatment, not explanation.
- **Required Beats:** Time jump to Jake at 22; unnamed rival challenges him; Jake wins with Cherokee tactics plus farm wrestling; village acknowledges earned status while he remains outsider; massive raid preparations open a future escape window.
- **Continuity In:** Jake is trained, has hidden supplies, and is inwardly preparing for possible escape.
- **Continuity Out:** Jake has earned respect but not belonging; the coming raid creates the conditions for Chapter 9's escape.
- **Style Rules:** Do not name the rival, do not make Jake fully accepted, do not start the Chapter 9 escape.
