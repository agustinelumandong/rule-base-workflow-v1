# Chapter 3 Drafting Plan

- **Target Word Count:** ~4,000 words.
- **Narrative Focus:** Third-person limited from Jake's perspective. The village should be understood through smoke, sound, work, glances, and physical treatment, not exposition.
- **Required Beats:** Harsh welcome; stripped and mocked as "Whispering Ash"; assigned to women's labor; first silent observation by Thorn Blackroot; daily grind and small acts of defiance; Thorn intervenes in a beating.
- **Continuity In:** Jake arrives at the mountain village exhausted, hungry, bound, and already watching captor routines.
- **Continuity Out:** Jake remains a low-status captive laborer, but Thorn Blackroot has noticed him and intervened once.
- **Style Rules:** No softened hierarchy, no affectionate Thorn, no ceremonial naming, no resolved family fate, no long translated speeches.
