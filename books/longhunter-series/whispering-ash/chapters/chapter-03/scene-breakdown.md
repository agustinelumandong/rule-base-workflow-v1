## BEAT 1: Named In Mockery

### Source Context Lock
- **Source Anchor:** phase-0: Chapter 3 - Harsh welcome: stripped, mocked as "Whispering Ash."
- **Continuity In:** Jake arrives at the mountain village exhausted, bound, hungry, and watched by captors.

## BEAT 3: Silent Elder

### Source Context Lock
- **Source Anchor:** phase-0: Chapter 3 - First meeting with Thorn Blackroot, who watches him silently.
- **Continuity In:** Jake is performing low-status camp labor under scrutiny.
- **Required Story Movement:** Thorn Blackroot observes Jake without affection or rescue, marking the first contact between them.

- Jake should notice Thorn's stillness, age, scars, and lack of wasted motion.

- **Required Story Movement:** Jake is beaten for defiance or failure under labor, and Thorn intervenes without softening the hierarchy.
