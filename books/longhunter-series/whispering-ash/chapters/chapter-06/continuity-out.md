# Chapter 6 Continuity Out

## Characters
- Jake Moses: Now 20. Trained under Thorn in ridge running, bow, knife, tomahawk, stealth, and ambush movement. Carries an ash-dyed camouflage cloak.
- Thorn Blackroot: Continues as strict mentor. Gives Jake serious weapons training but no firearms mastery.

## Locations
- Mountain ridges: Running and endurance training ground.
- Creek bend and timber targets: Bow, stealth, and sign-control training ground.
- Hide work area and fire pit: Site where Jake crafts and ash-dyes the cloak.
- Village edge: Men gather weapons for the first real combat test.

## Changes
- Time advances to Jake at age 20.
- Jake gains advanced practical skill with bow, knife, tomahawk, stealth movement, and ridge endurance.
- Jake crafts a buckskin cloak and dyes it with ash, turning the mocking name Whispering Ash into practical camouflage.
- A real combat test is imminent, but the raid itself has not begun.

## Human Stakes Carried
- Jake Moses: Carries the pressure of entering real violence with skills learned under captivity, not freedom.
- Jake Moses: Carries the cost of reclaiming Whispering Ash as cover while still living between settler origin and village training.
- Jake Moses: Carries the pressure to prove training under Thorn against men, not bark targets.
- Thorn Blackroot: Carries the cost of sending a trained captive into trail blood without pretending practice is the same as combat.

## Unresolved Pressure
- Jake has not mastered firearms and should not be written as a gunman yet.
- The first real combat test waits beyond the end of the chapter.
- Jake remains not fully accepted, though his skills are now serious.
