# Chapter 9 Drafting Plan

- **Target Word Count:** ~3,700 words.
- **Narrative Focus:** Third-person limited from Jake's perspective. Keep the escape physical, confused, and dangerous.
- **Required Beats:** Large raid collapses; Jake breaks away into timber; War Chief Red Hollow organizes immediate pursuit; Jake uses chaos, ash cloak, and stealth to slip past scouts; Jake ends alone and hunted in unfamiliar territory.
- **Continuity In:** Jake has hidden flint, dried meat, thorn needle, and sinew in his ash cloak.
- **Continuity Out:** Jake is free but hunted and not safe.
- **Style Rules:** Do not resolve Red Hollow's threat, do not make escape easy, do not give immediate safety.
