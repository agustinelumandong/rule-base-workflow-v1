# Chapter 4 Drafting Plan

- **Target Word Count:** ~4,200 words.
- **Narrative Focus:** Third-person limited from Jake's perspective. Keep winter survival physical: cold hands, weak legs, smoke, bark, wet wood, hunger, and labor.
- **Required Beats:** Brutal cold and near-starvation; Jake nearly freezes; Thorn saves him without comfort and begins basic teachings; Jake builds a better bark shelter using farm knowledge; small successes and quiet instructional conversations; Jake decides to learn aggressively.
- **Continuity In:** Jake is a low-status captive laborer, mocked as Whispering Ash, with Thorn having intervened once but not softened the hierarchy.
- **Continuity Out:** Jake remains captive, but Thorn's practical mentorship is established and Jake has a better winter shelter.
- **Style Rules:** No adoption, no ceremony, no sentimental Thorn, no family-fate resolution, no modern survival vocabulary.
