# Chapter 2 Drafting Plan

- **Target Word Count:** ~3,800 words.
- **Narrative Focus:** Third-person limited from Jake's perspective. Keep his understanding limited to what he can see, hear, smell, and physically endure.
- **Required Beats:** Harsh mountain trek; Jake helps an unnamed weaker captive; night camp threats and stories; Jake begins observing Cherokee travel methods and language patterns; arrival near the mountain village.
- **Continuity In:** Jake is bound, injured, and leaving his burned farm with family fate unknown.
- **Continuity Out:** Jake reaches the mountain village still captive, with early survival habits forming through observation.
- **Style Rules:** No modern terms, no explained psychology, no named weaker captive, no resolved family fate, no long translated Cherokee speeches.
