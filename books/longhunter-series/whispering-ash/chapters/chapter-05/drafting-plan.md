# Chapter 5 Drafting Plan

- **Target Word Count:** ~3,800 words.
- **Narrative Focus:** Third-person limited from Jake's perspective. Show skill growth through labor, weather, tracks, body change, and restrained village reactions.
- **Required Beats:** Time jump across years 2 to 4; Jake grows taller and leaner; joins hunting party despite skepticism; uses stealth and tracking to locate deer; helps bring down large prey; field dresses and carries meat; receives first limited praise; camp chores ease but equality does not arrive.
- **Continuity In:** Thorn's practical mentorship is established and Jake has chosen to learn aggressively.
- **Continuity Out:** Jake has proven useful on a deer hunt and is treated less like a slave, though he remains an outsider.
- **Style Rules:** No full acceptance, no adoption, no named extra hunters, no modern hunting language.
