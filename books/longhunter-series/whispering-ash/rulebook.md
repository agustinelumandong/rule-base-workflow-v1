## Characters
### Jake Moses (Whispering Ash)
- **POV Rules:** Strict third-person limited POV.
- **Physical Markers:** Wiry, sun-browned, long dark hair in wraps. Wears buckskins and ash-dyed cloak. Ties farm knots and holds rifle with a settler grip.
- **Voice:** Speaks in short, quiet, deliberate sentences.
- **Private Motive:** Survive independently on his own terms.

### Thorn Blackroot
- **POV Rules:** None (supporting character).
- **Physical Markers:** Scarred elder Cherokee in his 60s, traditional regalia.
- **Voice:** Deep, gruff. Speaks only to instruct.
- **Private Motive:** Pass down traditional hunting woodcraft.

### Caleb Thorne
- **POV Rules:** None (supporting character).
- **Physical Markers:** Mid-40s, full beard, squinting left eye.
- **Voice:** Dry, quiet frontier drawl.
- **Private Motive:** Live free of settlement expansion.

### War Chief Red Hollow
- **POV Rules:** None (supporting character).
- **Physical Markers:** Notched war axe.
- **Voice:** Commanding, formal.
- **Private Motive:** Protect tribal territory and punish Jake's escape.

### Winston McCrea
- **POV Rules:** None (supporting character).
- **Physical Markers:** Missing right index finger.
- **Voice:** Loud, boastful, abrasive.
- **Private Motive:** Profit from trapping and assert dominance.

### Anna Ray
- **POV Rules:** None (supporting character).
- **Physical Markers:** Silver ring on a cord around her neck.
- **Voice:** Flat, direct, businesslike.
- **Private Motive:** Keep her trading post secure and solvent.

## Chapter Continuity Ledger

### Chapter 1: The Raid
- **Required Story Movement:** Jake works on the farm, Cherokee raid destroys the farm, Jake is captured.

### Chapter 2: The Long March
- **Required Story Movement:** Jake is marched west into the Appalachian Mountains, learning sign and language to survive.

### Chapter 3: Pale Captive
- **Required Story Movement:** The captives arrive at the village; Jake is mocked as "Whispering Ash" and assigned camp labor under Thorn's gaze.

### Chapter 4: First Winter
- **Required Story Movement:** A hard winter brings starvation; Jake builds a bark shelter, and Thorn begins teaching him survival.

### Chapter 5: The Hunt - Years 2 to 4
- **Required Story Movement:** Jake participates in a successful deer hunt and gains acceptance.

### Chapter 6: Warrior Training - Years 5 to 6
- **Required Story Movement:** Jake trains as a warrior and crafts his ash-dyed camouflage cloak.

### Chapter 7: Blood on the Trail - Year 7
- **Required Story Movement:** Jake participates in a raid, kills a militia member, feels deep guilt (shown by cleaning his knife and avoiding gaze), and hoards flint and dried meat in his cloak.

### Chapter 8: Trial by Combat - Year 8
- **Required Story Movement:** Jake wins a trial by combat using farm wrestling, but remains isolated.

### Chapter 9: Chaos and Flight
- **Required Story Movement:** Jake escapes during a chaotic raid and evades pursuit.

### Chapter 10: River and Stone
- **Required Story Movement:** Jake travels alone, crosses a river, treats a thigh wound with pine resin, and forages for roots.

### Chapter 11: The Lone Scout
- **Required Story Movement:** Jake meets Caleb Thorne; they kill a charging black bear and form a wary partnership.

### Chapter 12: The Trading Post
- **Required Story Movement:** Jake and Caleb barter at Anna Ray's post; Jake ignores Winston McCrea's taunts and decides to leave.

### Chapter 13: The Gorge
- **Required Story Movement:** Jake selects a river gorge, maps defenses, and cuts the first log for his cabin.

### Chapter 14: Cabin in the Wild
- **Required Story Movement:** Jake builds his cabin, completes a long hunt, and prepares for winter as snow falls.
