# Research Pack

## Weapons & Ammo
- Colt Peacemaker: Single-action .45 caliber revolver, standard frontier handgun.
- Winchester 1873: Lever-action rifle, chambered in .44-40, highly common in the late 1870s and 1880s.
- Sharps Big Fifty: Single-shot buffalo rifle, .50-90 caliber, heavy range.

## Travel & Transport
- Stagecoach speed: Averages 5 to 8 miles per hour under normal conditions.
- Horse pace: Walk is ~3-4 mph, trot is ~8 mph, gallop is ~25-30 mph for short distances.

## Medicine & Survival
- Antiseptics: Carbolic acid (Lister's method) and iodine are used to clean wounds. No antibiotics exist.
- Pain Relief: Laudanum, morphia, and whiskey are typical pain relievers.

## Clothing & Gear
- Standard garb: Wool flannel shirts, canvas trousers, leather boots, Stetson or slouch hats.
