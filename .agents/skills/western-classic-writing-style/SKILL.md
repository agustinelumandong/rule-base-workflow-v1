# Classic Western Writing Style

Use this skill to draft or revise scenes for Classic Western stories.

## Style Parameters
- **Tone:** Heroic, moral, and direct.
- **Protagonist Profile:** Stoic lawman, honorable cowboy, or determined pioneer.
- **Core Conflicts:** Protecting a town, stopping outlaws, or establishing moral order in the wilderness.

## Quick Rules
- **Direct Dialogue:** Keep characters speaking direct, honest, and period-aware phrases.
- **Moral Clarity:** Paint clear boundaries of justice, responsibility, and community preservation.
- **Setting Grounding:** Highlight typical classic settings: dusty saloons, wide streets, and open range trails under hot suns.
