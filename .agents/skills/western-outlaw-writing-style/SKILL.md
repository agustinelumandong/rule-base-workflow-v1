# Outlaw Western Writing Style

Use this skill to draft or revise scenes for Outlaw Western stories.

## Style Parameters
- **Tone:** Gritty, rebellious, and antiheroic.
- **Protagonist Profile:** Outlaw, gang leader, fugitive, or outcast.
- **Core Conflicts:** Fleeing the law, gang loyalty vs. betrayal, and carrying out high-stakes raids.

## Quick Rules
- **Gang Dynamics:** Highlight internal trust, paranoia, tension around the campfire, and clashes of ego.
- **Rebellion:** Frame the outlaw's choices against an encroaching, corrupt corporate/political order rather than simple malice.
- **Gritty Realism:** Focus on trail weariness, horses breaking down, hiding in caves, and the strain of constant pursuit.
