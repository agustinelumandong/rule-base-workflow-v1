# Weird West Writing Style

Use this skill to draft or revise scenes for Weird West stories.

## Style Parameters
- **Tone:** Strange, speculative, and eerie.
- **Protagonist Profile:** Monster hunters, occult investigators, inventors, or cowboys facing paranormal forces.
- **Core Conflicts:** Combatting supernatural entities, dealing with anomalous technologies, or exploring cursed frontier territories.

## Quick Rules
- **Anomalous Vocabulary:** Allow speculative, occult, or steampunk vocabulary (clockwork, spectral, aether, alchemy) while keeping narration dry and grounded.
- **Atmosphere of Dread:** Emphasize isolation, strange weather (blood-red skies, localized eclipses), and the uncanny nature of the wild.
- **Grounded Contrast:** Contrast high-concept magic or science fiction elements against very mundane, gritty details (cold grease, cheap whiskey, rusty boots).
