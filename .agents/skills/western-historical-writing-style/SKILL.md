# Historical Western Writing Style

Use this skill to draft or revise scenes for Historical Western stories.

## Style Parameters
- **Tone:** Grounded, accurate, and restrained.
- **Protagonist Profile:** Ordinary people in extraordinary times, or figures inspired by real historical actors.
- **Core Conflicts:** Surviving documented historical events, resolving social/political tensions of the time, or navigating authentic frontier environments.

## Quick Rules
- **No Anachronisms:** Absolutely no modern jargon, idioms, or weapons. Double-check all material details.
- **Vivid Geography:** Use authentic historical names, maps, weather, and flora/fauna of the specified location.
- **Restrained Narration:** Describe everyday life, occupations, and processes (cattle branding, mining, rail laying) with technical precision.
