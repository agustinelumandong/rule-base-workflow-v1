# Frontier Romance Writing Style

Use this skill to draft or revise scenes for Frontier Romance Western stories.

## Style Parameters
- **Tone:** Emotional, resilient, and intimate.
- **Protagonist Profile:** Strong-willed homesteader, schoolteacher, rugged rancher, or independent business owner.
- **Core Conflicts:** Overcoming isolation, frontier hazards, or societal class divisions to build a shared life.

## Quick Rules
- **Shared Hardship:** Frame romantic interest through mutual struggle, cooperation, and character resilience.
- **Understated Intimacy:** Focus on subtle gestures (a shared campfire look, a firm clasp of hands) rather than melodramatic declarations.
- **Active Dialogue:** Give both partners strong voices and agency; avoid helpless maiden or generic savior tropes.
