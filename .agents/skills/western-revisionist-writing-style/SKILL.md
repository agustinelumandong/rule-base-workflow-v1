# Revisionist Western Writing Style

Use this skill to draft or revise scenes for Revisionist or Dark Western stories.

## Style Parameters
- **Tone:** Darker, morally complex, and deconstructive.
- **Protagonist Profile:** Flawed anti-heroes, disillusioned scouts, or complex minority actors.
- **Core Conflicts:** Brutal survival, systemic injustice, consequences of violence, and the disintegration of frontier myths.

## Quick Rules
- **No Simple Morality:** Avoid clean "good vs. evil" dynamics. Give all sides complicated motivations, flaws, and failures.
- **Realism of Violence:** Emphasize the physical, emotional, and social consequences of bloodletting. Action is fast, chaotic, and leaves lasting scars.
- **Grounded Subversion:** Subvert traditional heroic expectations; show the cost of greed, lawlessness, and expansion.
